BACKGROUND



TimeZone is a watch selling E commerce system which we developed out of curiosity to see how E commerce platforms operate.
- E commerce systems have lots of uses and they include -
1. Faster buying process.
2. Store and product listing creation.
3. Cost reduction.
4. Affordable advertising and marketing.
5. Flexibility for customers.
6. No reach limitations.
7. Product and price comparison.
8. Faster response to buyer/market demands.


PROBLEMS BEING SOLVED

Inflexibilty and Slower Buying Processes
